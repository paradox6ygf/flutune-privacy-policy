## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **Flutune** ("the Bot") collects, stores, and uses information when users interact with the Bot on Discord. By using the Bot, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide its services, the Bot may collect and store the following data:

* **Server IDs** – Used to identify and store server-specific settings.
* **Channel IDs** – Used for logging, announcements, and feature configuration.
* **Role IDs** – Used for auto-role systems, permissions, and server management features.
* **User IDs** – Used for moderation, user preferences, and bot functionality.
* **Moderation Data** – Warnings, punishments, logs, and related moderation records.
* **Configuration Data** – Server settings required for the Bot to operate correctly.
* **Message Content** – Only temporarily processed when required for commands, logging, moderation, or specific bot features.

## 2. How We Use Data

The collected data is used solely for operating and improving the Bot, including:

* Welcome and farewell messages
* Join/leave logging
* Moderation systems
* Auto-role assignment
* Server management features
* Music and entertainment features
* Custom server configuration
* Performance monitoring and bug fixing

## 3. Data Sharing

* The Bot does **not sell, rent, or trade** user data.
* The Bot does **not share personal data** with third parties except when required by law.
* Data is only accessible to the Bot owner and authorized maintainers for operational purposes.

## 4. Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable measures to protect stored information from unauthorized access, modification, disclosure, or destruction.

While we strive to protect all stored data, no system can guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The Bot relies on Discord's services and APIs and may utilize third-party APIs to provide certain features.

These services operate under their own privacy policies. The Bot does not intentionally share personal user information with third-party services beyond what is required for functionality.

## 6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the Bot's services and maintain functionality.

Unused or unnecessary data may be removed periodically at the discretion of the Bot owner.

## 7. Data Removal Requests

Server owners may request the deletion of their server's stored data.

To request data removal, please contact the Bot developer using one of the methods listed below.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not directed toward children under the minimum age required by Discord's Terms of Service.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Bot after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
